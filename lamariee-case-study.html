02 / The Client

About La Mariee Boutique

La Mariee Boutique is a luxury bridal boutique specializing in high-end designer wedding gowns. Their goal was to increase qualified bridal leads, drive appointment bookings, improve visibility in their regional market, and understand demand for emerging bridal designers.

The boutique was already running Google Ads campaigns, but needed clearer reporting, consistent optimization, and strategic insight beyond advertising metrics.

Recommendations Provided

Strong Potential Designers

Alexandra Grecco and Leah da Gloria showed stronger search visibility, better brand awareness, and more consumer demand indicators.

Niche Opportunity Designers

Lihi Hod and Suzanne Neville could still perform with localized marketing, bridal content campaigns, and paid search amplification.
